 II. Top 5 cities to visit

 1. Barcelona

Description

Barcelona is a vibrant cosmopolitan city located on the coast of northeastern Spain. It is renowned for its unique architecture, rich history, and lively atmosphere. The city offers a blend of beautiful beaches, captivating art and culture, and a thriving culinary scene.

TOP touristic attractions

  • Sagrada Familia: A breathtaking basilica designed by Antoni Gaudí
  • Park Güell: A whimsical park also designed by Antoni Gaudí
  • Gothic Quarter: A historic neighborhood with narrow streets, medieval architecture, and charming squares

National dishes

1. Paella: A flavorful rice dish with various ingredients like seafood, chicken, and vegetables.
2. Tapas: Small plates of various appetizers or snacks.
3. Crema Catalana: A traditional Catalan dessert similar to crème brûlée.

TOP accomodation ideas

  • Hotel Arts Barcelona: A luxurious hotel with stunning views of the sea
  • Casa Camper Hotel: A trendy boutique hotel in the heart of the city
  • Hotel Neri: A boutique hotel located in the Gothic Quarter with a rooftop terrace

Best season to visit

Spring and Fall

How to get there

Barcelona can be reached from Chisinau by flying to Barcelona-El Prat Airport, which has regular international connections. There are also options to travel by train or bus, but they involve multiple transfers and can be time-consuming.

 2. Madrid

Description

Madrid, the capital of Spain, is a bustling metropolis known for its historical landmarks, world-class museums, and vibrant street life. The city boasts a mix of old-world charm and modernity, with grand boulevards, picturesque parks, and an array of cultural experiences to enjoy.

TOP touristic attractions

  • Prado Museum: One of the world's premier art museums
  • Royal Palace: A stunning royal residence with beautiful gardens
  • Retiro Park: A vast park perfect for strolling, boating, and relaxing

National dishes

1. Tortilla Española: A Spanish omelette made with potatoes and eggs.
2. Jamón Ibérico: Cured ham from Iberian pigs.
3. Churros con Chocolate: Fried dough served with hot chocolate for dipping.

TOP accomodation ideas

  • The Westin Palace: A historic hotel known for its elegant interiors and central location
  • Gran Meliá Palacio de los Duques: A luxurious hotel near the Royal Palace
  • Hotel Ritz Madrid: A classic hotel with a beautiful garden and renowned serviceg

Best season to visit

Spring and Fall

How to get there

Madrid can be reached from Chisinau by flying to Madrid-Barajas Airport, which has direct flights from Chisinau. Alternatively, one can travel by train or bus, which usually involves multiple transfers and takes longer.

 3. Lisbon

Description

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a charming coastal city with a rich maritime heritage. It offers a combination of old-world charm and modernity, with its colorful neighborhoods, historical landmarks, and vibrant culinary scene. Visitors can explore its hilly streets, indulge in delicious seafood, and enjoy panoramic views of the Tagus River.

TOP touristic attractions

  • Belém Tower: A historic fortress overlooking the Tagus River
  • Alfama District: A maze of narrow streets, colorful buildings, and Fado music
  • Jerónimos Monastery: A magnificent monastery showcasing Manueline architecture

National dishes

1. Bacalhau à Brás: Shredded codfish with eggs, onions, and potatoes.
2. Pastéis de Nata: Custard tarts with a flaky crust.
3. Francesinha: A Portuguese sandwich with layers of meat, cheese, and sauce.

TOP accomodation ideas

  • Four Seasons Hotel Ritz Lisbon: A luxurious hotel with panoramic views of the city
  • Palácio Belmonte: A unique hotel set in a 15th-century palace
  • Memmo Alfama Hotel: A stylish boutique hotel in the heart of the Alfama district

Best season to visit

Spring and Fall

How to get there

Lisbon can be reached from Chisinau by flying to Lisbon Portela Airport, which offers international flights. Another option is to travel by train or bus, but it requires multiple transfers and can be time-consuming.

 4. Trieste

Description

Trieste is a captivating city located in northeastern Italy, close to the Slovenian border. Known for its unique blend of Italian, Austrian, and Slovenian influences, Trieste offers a charming old town, elegant architecture, and a picturesque waterfront. Visitors can soak up the city's cultural heritage, indulge in delicious cuisine, and enjoy stunning views of the Adriatic Sea.

TOP touristic attractions

  • Piazza Unità d'Italia: A grand square facing the sea
  • Miramare Castle: A beautiful castle surrounded by a scenic park
  • Canal Grande: A charming canal lined with colorful buildings and boats

National dishes

1. Prosciutto San Daniele: High-quality cured ham.
2. Scampi alla Busara: Shrimp cooked in tomato sauce.
3. Goulash Triestino: A flavorful meat stew with paprika and potatoes.

TOP accomodation ideas

  • Savoia Excelsior Palace Trieste: A historic hotel with elegant rooms and a seafront location
  • Hotel Continentale: A contemporary hotel with modern amenities
  • Hotel Victoria Trieste: A charming hotel with a central location and rooftop terrace

Best season to visit

Spring and Fall

How to get there

Trieste can be reached from Chisinau by flying to Trieste – Friuli Venezia Giulia Airport, which has limited international connections. Another option is to travel by train or bus, with possible transfers in other cities.

 5. Berlin

Description

Berlin, the capital of Germany, is a dynamic and cosmopolitan city known for its rich history, cutting-edge architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. It offers a fascinating mix of historical landmarks, world-class museums, trendy neighborhoods, and a thriving nightlife. Visitors can explore iconic sites like the Brandenburg Gate, visit world-renowned museums, and immerse themselves in the city's unique atmosphere.

TOP touristic attractions

  • Brandenburg Gate: A symbol of the city and a historic landmark
  • Museum Island: A UNESCO World Heritage site with several world-class museums
  • East Side Gallery: A section of the Berlin Wall covered in vibrant street art

National dishes

1. Currywurst: A popular street food dish consisting of sausage with curry ketchup.
2. Sauerbraten: A pot roast typically served with red cabbage and dumplings.
3. Berliner Pfannkuchen: A traditional Berliner doughnut with various fillings.

TOP accomodation ideas

  • Hotel Adlon Kempinski: An iconic luxury hotel near the Brandenburg Gate
  • The Ritz-Carlton Berlin: A five-star hotel known for its elegance and impeccable service
  • Generator Berlin Mitte: A stylish hostel with a vibrant atmosphere and budget-friendly options

Best season to visit

Spring and Summer

How to get there

Berlin can be reached from Chisinau by flying to Berlin Brandenburg Airport, which has international connections. Another option is to travel by train, with possible transfers in other cities along the way.
